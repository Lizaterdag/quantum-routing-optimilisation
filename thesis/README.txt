Introduction
============

Sample latex project for PhD dissertations based on an earlier version made by Tore Birkeland and Raymond Nepstad, modified and made available by Kjersti Birkeland Daae and Stephan Kral. This is not an official document. Visit https://avhandling.uib.no/en/ for official information on the thesis layout and requirements. For feedback pleas contact stephan.kral@uib.no
